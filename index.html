Why Campus Plug?

Built for Campus Life

We're not just another marketplace. We're the digital hub designed by students, for students — addressing the unique needs of Nigerian campus life.

Student-Verified Only

Every vendor is verified with their university credentials. No random sellers — just your trusted campus community.

Instant Campus Delivery

Meet on campus, get your items within minutes. No shipping fees, no waiting days — everything is just a hostel block away.

Student-Friendly Prices

No middlemen, no crazy markups. Deal directly with vendors who understand the student pocket.

In-App Chat & Bargaining

Message vendors directly, negotiate prices, and finalize deals — all within the app.

Your Own Digital Store

Vendors get a beautiful storefront with analytics, inventory management, and customer reviews.

Secure Payments

Pay safely with bank transfer, card, or our secure escrow system. Your money is protected until you confirm delivery.
